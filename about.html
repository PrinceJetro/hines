Why We Exist

African student deserves a fair shot

Too many brilliant students across Nigeria and the wider continent miss out on life-changing opportunities — not because of their ability, but because they lack access to the right guidance and connections.

Hines Education exists to close that gap. We combine deep knowledge of international admissions with personal, hands-on support — from your first enquiry to your first day on campus.

We are not an agency. We are your advocates, your translators of complexity, your partners in one of the most important decisions of your life.
