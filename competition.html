The Opportunity

Why Compete?

Three powerful reasons to step onto the stage

Meet University Representatives

Network directly with admissions officers from UK, US, and Canadian universities. Receive real-time feedback on your academic profile and study abroad prospects.

Win Life-Changing Prizes

Laptops, cash awards, championship trophies, and scholarship. Every finalist receives recognition — top performers change the trajectory of their education journey.

Get Noticed by Recruiters

Top performers are flagged directly to our partner institutions. Competition winners can receive direct admission offers and scholarship considerations.

The Process

How It Works

Four steps from registration to the championship stage

1

Register Online

Complete the registration form with your details, school, and preferred topic category.

2

Receive Your Topic

Your speech topic is sent via WhatsApp and email. You have two weeks to prepare.

3

Preliminary Rounds

Virtual or in-person heats at designated centres across Nigeria. Top performers advance.

4

Grand Finale

Championship stage at the International Education Fair. Meet university reps and win.
