Study in
Canada

Canada combines world-class education with one of the most generous post-study work policies globally. The Post-Graduation Work Permit (PGWP) lets you stay and work for up to 3 years after graduation — with a clear pathway to permanent residence through Express Entry.

  • PGWP: up to 3 years of work after graduation
  • Direct pathway to permanent residence via Express Entry
  • Co-op programmes integrate paid work during your degree
  • Multicultural cities — safe, welcoming environment for international students

Key Advantage

Post-Graduation Work Permit (PGWP)

Canada allows international graduates to work for up to 3 years after completing their degree — no job offer required. This is one of the most generous post-study work policies in the world, and it serves as a direct bridge to permanent residence through Express Entry.

Up to 3 years work permit after graduation
